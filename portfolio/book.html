Book Design

'Impact Culture' by Mark Reed

I designed this book was for Prof. Mark Reed, who is the founder of Fast Track Impact Ltd, and my current boss.

I created the artwork, and then I designed the book in Adobe InDesign. I wanted to emphasise how accessible the book is, so the illustrations have a hand-drawn appearance, and the chapter titles and headings use a font that I created myself using Mark's own hand-writing.
